New Aunoo situation report: competing influence campaigns over America's AI data centers.

In May, WIRED reported that Build American AI, a dark-money group tied to the pro-AI super PAC Leading the Future, paid social-media influencers to promote American AI and present China as the threat. In the weeks that followed, reports from the Bitcoin Policy Institute and Power the Future argued the reverse, that opposition to AI data centers is a China-linked foreign influence operation, and lawmakers cited them to seek FBI and DOJ investigations.

Based on the available public data, and in Aunoo's assessment, the two claims do not rest on the same evidence. The documented influence activity is on the pro-AI side. The counterclaim that opponents are foreign-driven is not supported by the current record: OpenAI found no meaningful breakout, and most data-center opposition is led by Republicans.
